redp is the release deployment platform for Nova releases. Use it to plan your release scope, collect evidence from Jira and GitLab, generate structured guideline reports, and reach deterministic GO / CONDITIONAL\_GO / NO\_GO approval decisions — all in one auditable workflow.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a release">
    Navigate to your Nova release in redp to see its current scope, components, and deploy plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a guideline report">
    Go to the **Nova Guideline** tab and trigger a new report. redp collects evidence from Jira, GitLab, and your component registry, then evaluates every readiness rule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the decision and blockers">
    Check the top-level approval decision — **GO**, **CONDITIONAL\_GO**, or **NO\_GO** — and drill into any blocking or conditional rules that require attention.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Act on required actions">
    Resolve blockers, file waivers for accepted exceptions, and obtain owner sign-offs until your release reaches a GO decision and is ready to proceed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
